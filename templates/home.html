About MeetMinder

MeetMinder is an innovative tool designed to simplify your meeting experience. It diarizes, transcribes, and summarizes recorded online meetings, saving your valuable time.

Key Features

  • Efficient summarization of meetings
  • Transcription of audio content
  • Identification of key discussion points
